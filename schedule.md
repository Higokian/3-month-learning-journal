## Emergency "Falling Behind" Recovery Plan

If you miss 2+ days or fall behind schedule:

1. **Don't panic** - it happens to everyone
2. **Assess realistically** - what can you catch up on?
3. **Prioritize** - focus on building projects over tutorials
4. **Adjust timeline** - add 1-2 weeks if needed
5. **Get back on track** - start with just 2 hours tomorrow

**Remember:** Consistency beats intensity. Better to code 4 hours daily than 12 hours twice a week.
